OVERVIEW:

Jump Puzzle - The idea was to create a 1st person 3D platforming puzzle with a "floor is lava" concept. If the player falls, the level is reset. The player must traverse obstacles, mostly involving jumping from platform to platform

    CONTROLS:
  • Left-click: move forward
  • Right-click: move backwards
  • Hold shift (while clicking): sprint
  • Space bar: jump
  • A, D keys: step left and step right
  • r: reset at starting position
    LAYOUT:
  • The final scene is "lava_scene", all other scenes were used to implement concepts before adding them to the final scene
    ASSETS:
  • A few materials and shaders were used from the Ultimate VFX pack https://www.assetstore.unity3d.com/en/#!/content/26701 (Alex had a paid copy of this package from a previous internship). Everything from this pack is in the "Mirza Beig" folder.
  • Many of the materials in the Resources folder were made by group members, but the rock textures were free from https://shop.bitgem3d.com/
    TECHNIQUES USED:
  • Basic physics: mass, gravity using Unity's engine
  • Various lighting techniques
  • shallow_wave from lab 5 was used to act as a lava floor
  • Collisions
  • Tranformation, rotation (of platforms and player character)
  • Particle Systems (portals)
